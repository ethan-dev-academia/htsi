We are showcasing HTSI's platform and Spring 2026 Online! We will talk about startups for students and provide resources for students and educators.

In 2026, the world is moving at a rapid pace: AI tools, remote collaboration, and global markets are accessible to anyone with any idea. Traditional education struggles to keep up, but founding a startup teaches unique problem solving, adapting teamwork and resilience in ways that textbooks can't. Attendees will learn why early exposure to real-world challenges accelerates learning, builds confidence, and develops a mindset for navigating complexity. This session is ideal for students and aspiring entrepreneurs and is two parts. First, we make the case that the startup journey in today's world is the most effective way to prepare for the future. Second, we discuss the current barriers to entry, limitations of this education technique and how to navigate in this field.
